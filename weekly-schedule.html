📅 Weekly Training Schedule

DAY 1 — Upper Strength DAY 2 — Sprint Intervals DAY 3 — Legs + Load DAY 4 — Easy Run DAY 5 — Gibush Simulation DAY 6 — Long Run DAY 7 — Simulation (AMRAP) / Rest

DAY 1 — Upper Strength

  • Pull-ups – 5 sets (max reps)
  • Push-ups – 5×20
  • Dips – 3×12
  • Towel hangs – 3×30 sec
  • Plank – 3×1 min

DAY 2 — Sprint Intervals

  • Warm-up jog – 5 min
  • 10× (100–150m sprint + walk back)
  • 3× hill sprints (all-out)

DAY 3 — Legs + Load

  • Walking lunges – 4×20 steps
  • Squats – 4×15
  • Step-ups – 3×12 each leg
  • Backpack carry (10–15kg) – 10–15 min

DAY 4 — Easy Run

  • 40–50 min easy run (can talk while running)

DAY 5 — Gibush Simulation

5 Rounds:
  • 10 burpees
  • 20 push-ups
  • 30 squats
  • 100m sprint
  • Crawl 20–30m
Rest 1–2 min between rounds

DAY 6 — Long Run

  • 6–12 km run (build gradually)

DAY 7 — Simulation (AMRAP)

20–30 Min Continuous:
  • 200m run
  • 15 push-ups
  • 15 sit-ups
  • 15 squats
  • 30 sec plank
Or rest if needed

🛠 Injury Prevention (2–3×/week)

  • Band pull-aparts – 15
  • External rotations – 12
  • Scapular retractions – 12

🎯 Skill Work (Add When Possible)

  • Crawling (bear + low crawl)
  • Sand training (run / push-ups / crawl)
  • Carry weight (10–20kg backpack)

📈 Targets

  • 12–18 pull-ups
  • 70+ push-ups
  • 10 km run
  • 60 sec hang

⚠️ Rules

  • Train consistently (don't skip days)
  • Don't go max every day
  • Sleep well
  • Eat enough
